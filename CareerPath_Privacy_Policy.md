# Privacy Policy — CareerPath

**Last updated: April 2026**

CareerPath is a structured career guidance mobile application designed to help students — particularly those in rural and semi-urban areas — explore academic and professional paths in an organized, hierarchical format. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

CareerPath stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- Student name
- Selected academic stream (Science, Commerce, or Arts)
- Affordability preference setting
- Browsing history of explored career paths
- Bookmarked career nodes
- App settings and theme preferences

**We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of this data.**

---

## 2. Data Storage

Your profile and preference data is stored **locally on your device** using SharedPreferences and SQLite. We do not:

- Upload your personal data to any server
- Sync your profile data to the cloud
- Transmit your personal information over the internet

The app does communicate with a backend API to fetch career information (streams, categories, institutes, books, job sectors), but **no personal user data is sent to the server** during these requests.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted from your device.

---

## 3. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your personal information to any third parties. No advertising, profiling, or user-tracking services are used in this app.

---

## 4. Analytics

The app uses **Firebase Analytics** (by Google) to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data — for example, which screens are viewed and which career paths are most explored. This data:

- Contains **no personally identifiable information (PII)**
- Cannot be traced back to any individual user
- Is used solely to improve the app experience and guide content priorities

You can review Google's privacy practices at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 5. Network & API Usage

CareerPath connects to a backend API to retrieve structured career data (streams, categories, career nodes, books, institutes, and job sectors). These API calls:

- Do **not** include any personally identifiable information
- Are cached locally (with a configurable TTL) to reduce unnecessary network requests
- Are used solely to deliver career guidance content to the user

---

## 6. Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Internet Access** | To fetch career data from the backend API |

No other device permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, photo library, etc.) are requested or required.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

CareerPath is designed for students of all ages, including those below 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. All profile data remains on-device and is never transmitted externally.

---

## 8. Data Security

Since all personal data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's own security (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device updated and secured.

Career data fetched from the API is cached locally and can be cleared at any time via the app settings.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app update notes and on this page. The "Last updated" date at the top of this document will be revised accordingly.

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
